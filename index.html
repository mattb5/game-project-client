Tic Tac Toe

Instructions

  1. If new to site, first complete sign-up form!
  2. Next sign-in with your newly created sign-up credentials
    • If a returning user, sign-in with your previously created sign-up credentials.
  3. Next, click "Start New Game" to start a new game in your game history.
  4. Enjoy a round of Tic-Tac-Toe with your friend. Good luck!
  5. To clear the board and start a new game, click "Start New Game" once again
  6. To view how many games you've played in your account, click the Get Games button
